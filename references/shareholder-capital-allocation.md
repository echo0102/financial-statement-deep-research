# Shareholder and Capital Allocation

This layer is optional. Add it after the financial core unless the user asks for it explicitly.

## What to Pull

- top holders and changes in position
- controlling shareholder or lack of one
- concert parties
- pledged shares
- buybacks and cancellations
- dividends and payout ratio
- equity incentives or share-based compensation
- dilutive issuance, convertibles, placements, or employee plans

## Interpretation Rules

- distinguish real control from simple concentration
- distinguish regulatory transfer or state-asset reorganization from discretionary selling
- do not infer manipulation from ownership concentration alone
- connect buybacks and dividends back to free cash flow and leverage

## Useful Questions

- is management returning capital from genuine surplus or from borrowed capacity?
- are incentives aligned with per-share value creation or only top-line growth?
- are insiders buying or selling in a way that changes the story?
