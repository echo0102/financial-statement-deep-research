# Note Analysis Playbook

Use this after rebuilding the statement base.

## Always Check

- accounting policy changes
- segment definitions and restatements
- non-recurring gains and losses
- related-party transactions
- pledges, covenants, guarantees, or contingent liabilities
- significant customers or supplier concentration
- share-based compensation and dilution
- tax items, deferred tax assets, uncertain tax positions

## Asset Quality

- receivable aging and expected credit loss assumptions
- inventory composition and write-downs
- goodwill and intangible impairment tests
- fair-value hierarchy and level 3 exposures
- capitalized costs and amortization policy

## Liability Quality

- debt maturity table
- variable-rate versus fixed-rate debt
- lease commitments
- pension or employee benefit obligations
- claims reserves, policy reserves, warranty reserves, or litigation reserves

## Cash Quality

- supplier financing or factoring
- non-cash gains embedded in earnings
- large swings in restricted cash
- capitalized interest

## Red-Flag Pattern

Escalate concern when several of these appear together:

- revenue growth up, cash conversion down
- profit up, receivables or inventories up faster
- leverage up, despite strong reported earnings
- repeated "fair value", "one-off", or "adjusted" explanations
- major changes in assumptions without a clear business reason
