# Valuation Playbook

Only do valuation after the accounting picture is clear.

## Core Rules

- do not apply the same multiple blindly across sectors
- use the metric the business model actually optimizes
- connect multiple choice to the statements you just analyzed

## Common Valuation Lenses

- industrial, consumer, internet:
  - P/E
  - EV/EBIT or EV/EBITDA
  - free-cash-flow yield
- banks:
  - P/B
  - ROE versus cost of equity
  - asset quality and capital buffers
- insurance:
  - P/B
  - P/EV
  - dividend yield
  - NBV and ROEV context

## Interpretation Rules

- a low multiple is not cheap if the statements show poor cash quality or hidden leverage
- a high multiple can still be rational if reinvestment quality and returns are durable
- if valuation and accounting quality disagree, explain which side is likely misleading
