# Industry Cycle Playbook

Industry-cycle analysis comes after the statements.

## Questions to Answer

- where is the industry in its supply, demand, pricing, and margin cycle?
- which parts of the company result are cyclical and which appear structural?
- what does the current cycle look like versus the last domestic cycle or a foreign analog?

## Evidence Order

1. company filings and segment disclosures
2. regulator and industry statistics
3. peer filings
4. high-quality external studies

## Common Signals

- utilization and capacity
- order book and backlog
- pricing and discounting
- inventory across the chain
- mix shift between premium and value products
- credit cost or claims cost by cycle stage
- policy or rate environment

## Warning

Do not let industry narratives excuse weak accounting quality. A cyclical rebound can coexist with poor cash conversion, weak disclosures, or risky leverage.
