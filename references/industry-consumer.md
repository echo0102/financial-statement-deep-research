# Consumer Industry Parameters

## Core Statement Extensions

- same-store sales or like-for-like growth
- volume, price, and mix
- channel mix
- inventory turnover
- promotional intensity
- gross-margin resilience

## Note Priorities

- distributor inventory
- returns and allowances
- brand amortization
- franchise or direct-store mix
- marketing capitalization or rebate treatment

## Cycle Lens

- consumption confidence
- price versus volume elasticity
- channel destocking or restocking
- premiumization versus downtrading
